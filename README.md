# E---Commerce-Sales-Dashboard-Power-BI-

🛒  E-Commerce Sales Dashboard (Power BI)

An interactive Power BI dashboard designed to analyze and visualize e-commerce sales performance. The dashboard provides insights into revenue, profit, order quantity, payment methods, customer purchasing trends, state-wise performance, and product category profitability.

📌 Project Objectives
Monitor overall sales and profit performance.
Analyze sales distribution across cities and states.
Track monthly profit trends.
Identify top-performing product categories and sub-categories.
Understand customer payment preferences.
Support data-driven business decisions through interactive visualizations.

🛠️ Tools & Skills Used :-

Power BI
Power Query
DAX
Data Modeling
Data Cleaning & Transformation
KPI Development
Business Intelligence
Data Visualization

State-wise Average Order Analysis
Monthly Profit Tracking
Payment Mode Distribution
Category & Sub-Category Profit Analysis
Interactive Filters and Slicers

💡 Key Insights :-

Furniture contributes the highest share of profit.
COD is the most preferred payment method.
Indore and Pune generate the highest sales.
Tables and Bookcases are the most profitable sub-categories.
Profit fluctuates significantly across months, highlighting seasonal trends.
